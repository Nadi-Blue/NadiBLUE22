Amazing Ocean Facts!

Did you know?

The ocean covers more than 70% of the Earth!

So many creatures!

The ocean is home to millions of different animals, from tiny plankton to giant whales.

Keeping our planet cool

The ocean helps keep our planet cool by absorbing heat from the sun.

The biggest animal ever!

The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth, and it lives in the ocean!

Tiny but mighty

Plankton are tiny ocean creatures, but they're super important for the ocean's food chain.

The ocean breathes for us!

The ocean produces much of the oxygen we breathe.

Underwater cities

Coral reefs are like underwater cities, full of colorful fish and other amazing creatures.

Marine mammals

Whales, dolphins, and seals are some of the amazing mammals that live in the ocean.

Fishy friends

The ocean is home to many different types of fish, like sharks, tuna, and salmon.

Invertebrates galore!

Jellyfish, squid, and octopuses are examples of invertebrates that live in the ocean.

Ocean plants

Seaweed and kelp are important plants that grow in the ocean.

The ocean is full of mysteries!

There's still so much we don't know about the ocean. It's a place of wonder and adventure.

The ocean helps us!

The ocean provides us with food, helps us travel, and is a great place to have fun!

Protecting our ocean

It's important to protect the ocean from pollution and overfishing so that it can continue to thrive.
